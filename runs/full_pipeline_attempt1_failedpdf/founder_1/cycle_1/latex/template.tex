\section{Experiments}
\label{sec:experiments}
The simple baseline CNN outperforms the pre-trained models, achieving 85.3\% accuracy while converging in 20 epochs, compared to 82.1\%, 83.4\% and 81.8\% accuracy for ResNet50, EfficientNet-B0 and DINOv2 respectively, which require 35-40 epochs to reach peak performance. These results suggest that transfer learning can sometimes hinder rather than help, challenging the prevailing wisdom that it is always beneficial in specialized domains.
